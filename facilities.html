    Guests can dine here looking out over the spectacular scenery from their private dining area which is right outside the guests' rooms. The menu includes a varied range of authentic Parsi cuisine. Occasionally, Chinese and variety of Indian dishes are also served.

Play Zone


    Our Playzone is a fantastic play centre, that's suitable for all ages! What sets our Playzone apart from the rest is that it really is suitable for all ages. We positively encourage all ages over 5yrs to have a go in the main play area. As well as being open all day every day for family fun. Playzone really is something different!

    • Children Play Area
    • Air(ice) Hockey
    • Beautiful Landscape Garden
    • Overlooking The Spectacular View
