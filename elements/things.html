Places to visit

    Name Description Category Sub-Category Distance(Kms)
    Table Top Land Table Top Land is a mountain pleateau at Panchgani and where tourists converge in the evening for some rides and views. One of the unique aspect of this pleateau is that nothing ever seems to grow here. View Point, Rides Valley Views, Horse Riding 1
    Parsi Point Parsi Point in Panchganj offers excellent views of the Krishna Valley View Point Valley Views 2
    On Wheelz On Wheelz is an amusement park in Panchgani that offers all kinds of rides for kids and adults. Theme Parks Amusement Park 2
    Sydney Point Sydney Point at 3 kms from Panchgani offers views of the Krishna Valley, Kamalgad Fort and area around. View Point Valley Views 3
    Sherbaug A Kind of Amusement park in Panchgani that is popular with families having kids Theme Parks Amusement Park 3
    Mapro Garden Mapro garden is set on Mahabaleshwar - Panchgani road around Mapro Jam factory where one arrives for purchasing all kinds of local produce such as Strawberry Jams, crush etc. The snack counter outside that serves sandwiches and shakes is also very popular. Shopping General 7
