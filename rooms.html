Rooms and Suites

The hotel has a variety of rooms ranging from couple standard rooms to Suite Rooms. many of the rooms are Valley - Facing and customers can enjoy the scenic view of the verdant mountains, valley and waterfalls from the privacy of their rooms. Most rooms are fitted with A/cs. All Rooms have LED TVs. All the rooms have hot and cold water 24 hours.
